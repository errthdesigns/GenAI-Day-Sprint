# Ad Sprint - Quick Start Guide

## Your First Brief

1. **Read the Brief**
   - Use arrow buttons to navigate through pages
   - "Start" button appears after reading all pages

2. **Start Working**
   - Click "Start" to begin 10-minute countdown
   - Create your design in your tool of choice

3. **Submit Your Work**
   - Click "Submit" button
   - Upload files (optional)
   - Click "Done" button

4. **Enter Your Details** (First time only)
   - Enter **Your Name** (e.g., "Sam")
   - Enter **Team Names** (e.g., "Erin, Vi")
   - Click "Done" ✓

5. **Submit URL**
   - Paste your design link (Figma, Drive, etc.)
   - Click "Submit"

6. **Watch the Client Reaction**
   - Faster = Better reaction!
   - Next brief loads automatically

## Subsequent Briefs

Same flow, but you'll only enter **Team Names** (step 4) - your name is already saved!

## Quick Tips

- **Toggle Views**: Use bottom buttons to switch Brief/Timer/Submit
- **Speed Matters**: < 3min = surprised, 3-7min = happy, > 7min = frown
- **Help Available**: Click Help button (bottom right) anytime
- **Press X**: Debug shortcut to trigger timeout immediately

## Time's Up?

If timer hits 0:00:
- Screen shakes → "OUT OF TIME!"
- Click "Start Again" to retry

## Your Name Display

After first submission, your name appears in top-right corner throughout the session.
