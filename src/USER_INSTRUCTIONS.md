# Ad Sprint - User Instructions

Welcome to Ad Sprint! This is a 10-minute creative challenge where you'll respond to design briefs under time pressure.

## Getting Started

1. **Open the Ad Sprint app** in your web browser
2. **Enter your name** when prompted (this will be used to save your submissions)
3. Click **"Start"** to begin

## How It Works

### 1. Read the Brief
- You'll see a multi-page brief describing a design challenge
- Use the arrow buttons to navigate between pages
- Read carefully - the last page shows the requirements (Outcome)
- The **Start** button will activate once you've read all pages

### 2. Start the Timer
- Click **Start** to begin your 10-minute countdown
- The timer will appear in the top center
- You can switch between viewing the **Brief** and **Timer** using the bottom-left button

### 3. Create Your Design
- You have 10 minutes to create your response
- Work in your preferred design tool (Figma, Photoshop, etc.)
- Upload your design to a shareable location (Figma, Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.)

### 4. Submit Your Work
- Click **Submit** (bottom center) when you're ready
- You can upload files if needed (optional)
- Click **Done** when you're ready to submit
- **Paste the URL** to your design in the dialog that appears
- Click **Submit**

### 5. Get Feedback
- Watch the animated client reaction
- You'll receive instant feedback based on your completion time
- The next brief will load automatically

## Tips

- **Read the entire brief** before starting the timer
- **Work quickly** - the client reaction depends on your speed:
  - Under 3 minutes: Surprised (wow!)
  - 3-7 minutes: Grin (good job!)
  - Over 7 minutes: Frown (keep practicing!)
- **Use the Help button** (bottom right) if you need guidance
- **Stay calm** - it's meant to be intense but fun!

## Time Limits

- **10 minutes total** per brief
- If time runs out, you'll see a timeout screen
- Click **"Start Again"** to retry the same brief

## Keyboard Shortcuts

- **Arrow keys**: Navigate brief pages (when viewing brief)
- **Enter**: Submit URL (when in submit dialog)

## Your Submissions

All your submissions are automatically saved with:
- Your name
- The brief you completed
- Your completion time
- The URL you submitted
- Timestamp

Your instructor/organizer will have access to review all submissions.

## Need Help?

Click the **Help** button (bottom right) during any brief for additional guidance.

## Have Fun!

Remember, this is a learning exercise. The goal is to:
- Practice working under pressure
- Improve your speed and decision-making
- Get comfortable with quick ideation
- Learn from the feedback

Don't worry if you don't finish in time - each brief is a chance to improve!
